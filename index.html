Explore North Myrtle Beach

From sandy shores to lively entertainment, our prime location offers the best of the Grand Strand at your doorstep.

The Beach

Enjoy the sun and surf! You're just a pleasant five-minute walk from the beautiful, wide-open sands of North Myrtle Beach.

Barefoot Landing

A must-visit hub on the Waterway for premier shopping, dining (Greg Norman's), and live shows at the Alabama Theatre.

Boardwalk & SkyWheel

Head south to the most iconic landmark in the area. The views from the top of the 200-ft SkyWheel are breathtaking.

Broadway at the Beach

A family paradise featuring Ripley's Aquarium, WonderWorks, unique shops, and dozens of fantastic restaurants.

Cherry Grove Pier

Spend an afternoon fishing, strolling, or simply enjoying the ocean views from this historic and scenic local landmark.

Alligator Adventure

One of the largest reptile zoos in the world! A fantastic and memorable outing, especially for families with kids.

Duplin Winery

A relaxed adult activity offering tastings of their famous Muscadine wine in a large, beautiful space. A great change of pace.

Main Street Entertainment

Experience the local flavor with classic arcades, shag dancing clubs, and beach shops that define North Myrtle Beach.
